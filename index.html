Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I’ve got answers. Here’s everything you need to know before booking your build.

How do you figure out what I want if I’m not even sure myself?

I’ve developed a personality-style questionnaire to help clients clarify their brand vision. It’s my job to translate your ideas into a clean, effective website — even if you don’t know how to describe it yet.

What’s your average turnaround time?

Depends on the tier. For landing pages, I can typically deliver within 3 days after the questionnaire is completed. Full websites take 1–2 weeks. eCommerce builds take about 2–3 weeks. Revision requests can extend timelines slightly.

I don’t have a logo. Can you help?

Yes! I offer logo design and branding as add-ons. Or I can place a temporary placeholder logo until you’re ready.

Will I own the website once it’s finished?

Absolutely. You’ll receive full access and instructions on how to manage your site.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. I typically do a 50/50 split — 50% upfront, 50% upon delivery.

How many revision rounds are included?

Landing pages come with up to 10 revision sessions, full websites get 25, and eCommerce sites include 15. I’ll always be clear if something is going outside scope before we continue.

Can I update the website myself later?

Yes. Your site will come with login credentials and a guide to help you edit or manage content yourself.

Will my site be mobile-friendly?

Of course. Every design is mobile-first and responsive across all modern devices.

Can you help me write the text/copy?

Yes — I offer full copywriting services as an add-on. I can help craft compelling headlines, call-to-actions, and more.

What platform do you build on?

I build all websites using self-hosted WordPress across every tier. It gives me the flexibility to use custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript where needed — while still giving you the option to manage your site with a user-friendly dashboard. For eCommerce builds, I use WooCommerce.
