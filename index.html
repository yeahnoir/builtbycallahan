What I Do

I design landing pages and mini websites that help small business owners build a real online presence. Whether you're a barber, realtor, stylist, or running a niche shop — if you need a site that actually looks good and works, I’ve got you covered.

This isn’t some quick Wix template or a Fiverr rush job. I take time with every build because I know how overwhelming it is to run a business and try to piece together a decent website. That’s where I come in — so you can focus on your craft while I build the online space your brand deserves.

Why It Matters

Social media only shows so much — and it all looks the same. A custom landing page gives you creative freedom to express your brand and connect with customers in a way Instagram never could.

In 2025, if people can’t find you online, they won’t trust you. With the right site, you build credibility, boost conversions, and create email capture systems that grow your customer base for the long run.
