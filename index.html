Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't know what I want in a website?
I walk you through a simple personality questionnaire to help uncover your vision. It’s my job to turn your ideas into a clear, stunning site — even if you’re unsure how to describe it.
How fast will my site be finished?
Turnaround depends on the package. Landing pages: ~3 days after questionnaire. Full websites: 1–2 weeks. Ecommerce: 2+ weeks. Revisions and my workload may affect timing.
What if I don’t have a logo?
I offer logo design as an add-on or can use a professional placeholder until you’re ready. I also offer full branding support if needed.
Will I have access to my site?
Yes. You’ll receive full access with instructions on how to update content, add images, or request help.
Do you use WordPress?
Yes. All of my builds are based on WordPress for long-term flexibility. I also use custom code where needed, especially for high-performance landing pages and animations.
How do revisions work?
Each package includes a revision cap: 10 for landing pages, 15 for ecommerce, and 25 for full websites. Most people never hit the cap.
Can I request copywriting?
Yes, you can add professional copywriting as an optional service. I’ll handle it for you based on your brand’s voice and goals.
Will it work on mobile?
Absolutely. All websites are fully mobile responsive, optimized for speed and clarity on any device.
